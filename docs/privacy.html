Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Vaultic ("the App") is a local-first asset manager built for iOS. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. No Data Collection

Vaultic does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is no backend server, no account system, and no analytics SDK. All data you enter (assets, debts, settings) is stored exclusively on your device in a local SQLite database and never leaves your device.

2. Biometric Authentication

The App offers an optional app-lock feature using Face ID or Touch ID. Biometric data is processed entirely by the iOS operating system (LocalAuthentication framework) and is never accessed, stored, or transmitted by Vaultic.

3. Price Data (Network Requests)

To display current precious-metal and cryptocurrency prices, the App makes outbound requests to public, free-tier APIs (gold-api.com, coingecko.com). These requests contain no personal identifiers — only the asset symbol and currency code. No account or device ID is sent. Responses are cached locally for 15 minutes to minimise network usage.

4. Local Storage Only

All application data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device (oam.db). No data is uploaded to any cloud service. Deleting the App permanently removes all associated data.

5. Third-Party SDKs

Vaultic does not include any advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting SDKs. The only outbound network traffic is the price-data requests described in Section 3.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
